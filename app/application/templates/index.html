The One-word Turing Test is a minimal adaptation of the standard Turing Test in which an artificial intelligence has to convince a human that it is in fact a human too. In our version we will pit man against machine and ask you to guess which word was generated by AI. Keep an eye on WDSS and PsychSoc to see our talk analysing the results of this experiment in a few weeks' time.
