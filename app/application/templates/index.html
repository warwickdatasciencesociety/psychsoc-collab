One-Word Turing Test

Select the word you think is from a human.

The One-Word Turing Test is a minimal adaptation of the standard Turing Test in which an artificial intelligence has to convince a human that it is in fact a human too. In our version we will pit man against machine and ask you to guess which word was submitted by a human rather than an AI. Keep an eye on WDSS and PsychSoc to see our talk analysing the results of this experiment in a few weeks' time.
