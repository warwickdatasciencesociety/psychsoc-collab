One-Word Turing Test

Submit a Word

Submissions need to be approved by a moderator before they will appear in the application. The rules for submissions are as follows:

  • Enter a single word containing only letters, hyphens, and apostrophes
  • A word does not have to be real nor spelled correctly
  • Derogatory language will not be permitted but crudeness within reason is
  • Do not include any personally identifying information
  • You are free to repeat a word already used in the app

We will use the provided email address only to contact you at the end of the project to share how your word ranked. You are free to include your name for inclusion on a public leaderboard released at the end of the project, or you can leave this blank to submit anonymously. We store these details in accordance with the data collection policy linked in the footer.
